agricultural south with high unemployment <\s>
<s> the italian economy is driven in large part by the manufacture of high-quality consumer goods produced by small and medium-sized enterprises many of them family owned <\s>
<s> italy also has a sizable underground economy which by some estimates accounts for as much as 15 of gdp <\s>
<s> these activities are most common within the agriculture construction and service sectors <\s>
<s> italy has moved slowly on implementing needed structural reforms such as reducing graft overhauling costly entitlement programs and increasing employment opportunities for young workers particularly women <\s>

<s> italy has moved slowly on implementing needed structural reforms such as reducing graft overhauling costly entitlement programs and increasing employment opportunities for young workers particularly women <\s>
